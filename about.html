About Me

Jamie Salinger is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Stage Management from the University of Southern California’s School of Dramatic Arts. Originally from Florida, Jamie graduated from Alexander W. Dreyfoos High School of the Arts where she stage managed countless mainstage and black box productions. In her senior year, Jamie was named “Best Overall Dreyfoos Thespian” and “Dreyfoos Theatre Department Standout Senior.”

She went on to become a Trustee Scholarship Recipient at USC and has since joined both the Trojan Scholar’s Society and the Thematic Option Honors Program. In her first two years at USC, Jamie has worked on the stage management team of seven productions at the University of Southern California, served as Production Manager of USC’s Musical Theatre Repertory, worked professionally as both a PSM and ASM for the Musical Theatre Guild of Los Angeles, and served as a PA at Irish Repertory Theatre in NYC. She looks forward to the exciting and challenging future that the world of stage management will bring her.
